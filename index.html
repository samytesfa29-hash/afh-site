Our Philosophy

Clinical Excellence in a Family Home

At Peaceful Harmony, we believe that aging with dignity shouldn't mean sacrificing the comforts of home for clinical safety.

Founded and managed by Aster Birri, a Registered Nurse, our Beaverton residence bridges the gap between institutional facilities and independent living. By consciously limiting our home to just five residents, we ensure a staff-to-resident ratio that allows for true personalized care.

We don't just manage medications; we know our residents' favorite stories. Here, professional health oversight is woven seamlessly into daily life.

Comprehensive Care

RN-Managed oversight for complex needs.

Health Management

Rigorous oversight of medications (including insulin), doctor appointments, and daily vitals monitoring by Aster Birri, RN.

Nutrition & Dining

Three home-cooked, balanced meals daily. We accommodate diabetic, low-sodium, and texture-modified diets.

Daily Support

Respectful assistance with bathing, dressing, and grooming. A safe, accessible environment for mobility needs.
